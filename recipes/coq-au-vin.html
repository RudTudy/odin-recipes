Coq Au Vin Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken(Dark meat preferred)
  • 750ml Red Wine
  • 3 Carrots, Diced
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • 1L beef broth
  • Lots of bacon
  • Mushrooms
  • Pearl Onions

Directions

  1. Cook the bacon in your dutch oven until golden
  2. Remove bacon and cook chicken in bacon fat until golden
  3. Set chicken aside and deglaze the pot with the wine
  4. After half of the wine has evaporated, pour the beef broth, add the carrots, garlic and let simmer for 45 mins
  5. In the meantime,cook the mushrooms until browned.
  6. Finish off the chicken in the oven for about 10 mins at 400 degrees and add to the wine/broth mixture
  7. About 5 mins before plating, sautee the pearl onions in butter and add to the mixture
  8. Voila, You are done!
